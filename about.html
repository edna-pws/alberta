About Us

At AlbertaIT we believe in simplistic yet innovative Managed IT solutions. Our services are designed with your IT goals and vision in mind. We partner with your organization and take the time to understand the full scope of your requirements and work with you to deliver an innovative, efficient IT solution which improves overall organizational efficiency, saves time and money and enables business growth.

We articulate clearly at each stage of a project to ensure your organization has a full understanding of the scope, requirements and solution to make each Managed IT services solution a success.AlbertaIT is a leading provider of IT Services and Solutions throughout Alberta. Our expertise includes: IT Support(network, server and desktop), Helpdesk and Remote Support, Onsite Support and Troubleshooting, Virtualization, Threat Management, Disaster Recovery services and much more.

Just like larger companies, small and medium - sized businesses need technology to operate efficiently and to compete effectively in the global marketplace. As your reliance on IT grows,the resources to support an increasingly complex business may not yet exist within your company. In many small and medium - sized businesses, IT resources are scarce, and can be quickly overwhelmed with the day - to - day responsibilities of keeping the IT infrastructure up and running that the business depends on.

This is why it can be more cost efficient to outsource your technology needs to a managed service provider such as AlbertaIT. As your trusted IT advisor, we assume ongoing responsibility for monitoring, securing, hosting and / or problem resolution for your IT systems. We handle every aspect of your computer system, so you don’ t have to.

With our customized Managed IT Services, you really can rely on us to provide everything you need to keep your IT up and running. Get proactive IT support you can count on, no matter what. Our clients can testify for the commitment to quality, sophistication and ability to deliver cost - effective solutions in minimal time. Entrust your business infrastructure with AlbertaIT.

Jeremy as the Director of Consulting has been an integral part of the AlbertaIT’s executive team and a key contributor to the company’s successful growth by his overriding focus and pride in understanding the needs of AlbertaIT’s end users and successfully delivering products and services that exceed client expectations. Michael and Jeremy’s experience includes over twenty-five years in the information technology profession. They are both IT veterans with experience that encompasses developing, selling, and implementing a broad range of IT solutions for clients across virtually all business segments.

Michael’s overriding focus has been in exceeding client expectations by providing the most comprehensive and highest quality IT managed services available to small businesses in Alberta. Michael is the foundation behind our satisfied clients exemplified by his combination of technical aptitude, strong account management experience and open communication style. He created and maintains a structure and procedures that ensure the highest level of accuracy and efficiency, focusing on transparent client communications and accountability. Michael overseas top accounts and projects and is part of the research and development team for new products and services. It is Michael’s dedication to client relations that is the key to AlbertaIT’s successful growth, market presence, and exceptional client satisfaction.

AlbertaIT staff is made up of experienced and certified professionals dedicated to providing excellence in both technical and customer service.

Our IT Support team provides telephone and remote helpdesk services to our Complete Care clients from our headquarters. We utilize industry leading endpoint management systems and documentation.

Our network administration team provides our onsite support services which consist of proactive IT checklist, best practices audits and reporting, network documentation, site security, new computer, laptop, and printer installations.

Our centralized services team manages and maintains the core of our client networks. This includes 24/7 monitoring of client networks, patching and software maintenance, as well as security implementation.

Our IT management team is made up of seasoned IT professionals who act as a main point of contact and help our clients from a business perspective with their IT networks. This includes short term and long term planning and budgeting, monthly, quarterly and annual updates, detailed annual reviews, and ongoing consulting and guidance to help our clients make smart IT decisions.

Our projects team assists clients with changes to their infrastructure and is credentialed in many dependencies including Microsoft, VMWare, and many others. Some typical projects include: server installations/upgrades, server virtualization, office moves, data center implementation, cloud implementation, small and large workstation rollouts, and new office deployment.

Our IT consulting and technology support services will give you the satisfaction you deserve. You can entrust our IT management services for a successful outcome with no hassles whatsoever.
